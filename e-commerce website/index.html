ABOUT US

ONE CAGE LEAGUE (OCL) is one of the biggest MMA GYM in India. The founder and active Chief Instructor of the gym is Sensei MANISH KUMAR, who has been training KARATE, MMA, GRAPPLING, BJJ, JUDO & KICK BOXING for over three decades. He holds the rank of (6th degree black belt) & Blue Belt (Brazilian Jiu –Jitsu) under Professor Olavo Abreu (Carlson Gracie lineage) of Phuket Top Team. . Sensei Manish Kumar represents Sanshinkan Martial arts in Delhi INDIA , He also participated at Grappling world championship at Belarus in 2016. He has been successfully leading the Grappling Federation of Delhi as General Secretary and has been a key member in guiding Grappling Federation of India. he has been a trainer in indian Army (Rajputana Regiment) over a year, He also has been a Trainer in Delhi Police. Sensei Manish Kumar is dedicated to promote Martial Arts in all over India he trained among Security Personal in Corporate & Private Sector. Sensai Manish Kumar producing champion players, through his training and Championships. One Cage League use to organise Championships and Leagues regulary to produce Champions and for a good platform for Young Martial Artis and atheletes. Many of Manish Kumar trained students represented India in highly reputed International level Championships held every year. ONE CAGE LEAGUE (OCL) is a flexible BODY of Martial art Championships that Produduces numerous of Champions in india and it’s a unique platform for international championships. One Cage League (OCL) gym includes people of all ages and nationalities. We offer separate batches for Kids, and Girls. New students are welcome to join the gym at any time If you interested to train with us you are very welcome anytime. Just show up for any training session.
